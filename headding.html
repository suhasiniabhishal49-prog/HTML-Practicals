>HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the standard language for creating and structuring content on the web. It uses elements and tags to define headings, paragraphs, lists, links, images, and other resources, and works together with CSS and JavaScript to build accessible, interactive web pages.

CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is the language used to control the presentation of HTML content—defining colors, fonts, spacing, and layout (including Flexbox and Grid) and enabling responsive designs with media queries so sites look good across devices while keeping style separate from structure.

Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 1 beef patty (about 150–200g)
  • 1–2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 burger bun, toasted
  • Salt, pepper, and a little oil or butter
  • Optional: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard, mayo

Instructions

  1. Season the patty with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and add a little oil or butter.
  3. Cook the patty 3–4 minutes per side for medium (adjust time for doneness).
  4. Place cheese on the patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  5. Toast the bun lightly on the skillet or grill.
  6. Assemble: bottom bun, patty with cheese, desired toppings, sauces, and top bun. Serve hot.
