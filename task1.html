Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 200 g ground beef (or two 100 g patties)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • 1 burger bun
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Optional: lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup, mustard

Preparation

  1. Form the ground beef into a patty about 1–2 cm thick; season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patty 3–4 minutes per side for medium (adjust time for preferred doneness).
  3. Place the cheese slice on the patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  4. Toast the bun briefly in the skillet if desired, then assemble: bun, patty with cheese, and desired toppings and sauces.
